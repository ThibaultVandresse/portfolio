About Me

My name is Thibault Vandresse

I am a game designer, I have studied 4 years in game design and development.

List of software I can use:

  • Blender / Maya
  • Unity
  • Vegas Pro
  • Photoshop

Game Design and Story writing is my passion!

I have always been really creative, and the main media I want to create on is the video game media.

I write stories all the time, and I hate to keep them all to myself. I want to have others experience them.

For that purpose, I focused on game creation, programming and game design.

Creating a good game is not just about having a good story or good gameplay. It’s about the experience and how well the game delivers on that.

I want to give unique experiences to the player with the best of my abilities.

My Style in Games

In terms of art style, I tend to go for a more cartoon/simple style.

Because of the contrast it creates with the themes of the games that I make, going into mature subjects, and also to help with the humor of said games.

In general, I am more focused on making games centered on exploration and completion.

Since every game I make rewards you for replaying and finding everything in a level.
